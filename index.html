How it works?

This application requires valid information. The user is required to register in the app using their correct student number and other details.

Colegio De Montalban assigns the available tree planting area. Upon registration, users will be given a choice to choose their desired location.

A step-by-step instruction will be given to the student in preparing for tree planting seeds. Such as inquiring the seed from Colegio De Montalban campus.

If all necessary things are prepared. The user will be given a step-by-step instruction with video tutorial to help them succeed. First step will be a selfie with the seed and signboard with their name on it. The next step will be a 20 seconds video recording of them planting the seed. The last step is to reconfirm if the details are correctly done. If not, they are allowed to redo the step.

About

Our tree planting system provides the tool to ensure that the Tree Planting Graduation Requirement is a success and that the natural environment is protected for years to come.

Our job is to make things easier, we are developing this tool to help Colegio De Montalban Tree planting successful and aim for CDM students to actively participate in conservation efforts.

Helping environment is a cool thing to do. Our ultimate goal is to provide a tool for the CDM to use in implementing environmental protection efforts.
